History of Our Company

Our company was first started by Ali Muhmmad Sargarth, the founder of Sargath Industries, in partnership with a Marwadi person. After some time, they parted ways to expand individually, both achieving success. Iqbal Bhai Sargath, the son of Ali, took over the business and drove M.I Brass Industries to become a symbol of excellence. Years later, Mr. Asfak Sargath, son of Mr. Iqbal, took control and faced challenges like demonetization, GST, and the COVID-19 pandemic. Despite these difficulties, he kept the company afloat and later revitalized the business, leading it to success again.

About Company

Currently, our company is under the leadership of Mr. Asfak Sargath, a visionary businessman who navigated many challenges to restore the company's prosperity. M.I Brass Industries manufactures various products, including cable glands and push buttons. We are committed to delivering high-quality products that meet industry standards. Mr. Asfak himself oversees product inspections to ensure quality, and our company strives to deliver orders on time for the satisfaction of our clients.
